\subsection{Detailed Description}
Functions for D\+FT and I\+D\+FT. 

In mathematics, the discrete Fourier transform (D\+FT) converts a finite sequence of equally-\/spaced samples of a function into a same-\/length sequence of equally-\/spaced samples of the discrete-\/time Fourier transform (D\+T\+FT), which is a complex-\/valued function of frequency.
